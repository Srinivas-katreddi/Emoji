EVERY HAPPY FACE HIDES A SAD FACE
Smiles can hide so much;fear,pain,sadness,tears.
            But then again they reflect one thing: strength.
      Even a happy life cannot be without 
            a measure of darkness, and the word happy 
            would lose its meaning if it were not 
            balanced by sadness. It is far better take 
            things as they come along with patience and
             equanimity.
